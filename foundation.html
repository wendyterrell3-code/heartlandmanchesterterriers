Foundation Dogs

Falkor

GCH Kairos Lucky Dragon at Heartland TKN, BCAT

Health Tested Stud Dog Manchester Terrier

Falkor is a Grand Champion male who combines correct structure, balanced movement, and a steady, thoughtful temperament. He is a moderate, well-constructed dog with clean lines, strong topline, and efficient movement that holds up in the ring. Falkor consistently demonstrates soundness and composure, bringing confidence without excess and presence without noise.

He is a proven representative of the breed standard and was selected as a foundation dog with long-term preservation in mind. Falkor’s strengths lie in his overall balance, smooth gait, and ability to produce puppies with correct bodies and stable temperaments.

Pedigree & Background

Falkor comes from a pedigree selected for correct structure, sound movement, and long-term breed preservation. His lineage reflects dogs known for clean outlines, efficient gait, and stable temperaments rather than extremes or short-term trends.

The breeding behind Falkor emphasized consistency and functionality, producing dogs capable of holding structure over time while maintaining breed type. These influences are evident in Falkor’s balance, smooth movement, and composed presence both in and out of the ring.

Falkor was chosen as a foundation dog not only for his individual merit, but for the reliability of the pedigree behind him and its ability to complement future breeding decisions within the Heartland program.

Falkor has completed comprehensive health testing appropriate for the Standard Manchester Terrier. Thyroid testing is currently pending and will be updated upon completion.

Evie

CH Kairos Magic Mirror at Heartland BCAT

Health Tested Foundation Bitch Manchester Terrier

Evie is a Champion female who brings balance, femininity, and thoughtful presence to the Heartland program. She is a well-constructed bitch with correct proportions, clean lines, and efficient movement, maintaining breed type without exaggeration.

Evie is steady and observant by nature, showing confidence without excess and a calm, sensible temperament. Her structure and attitude allow her to move freely and efficiently, with the kind of consistency that holds up both in the ring and in a breeding program.

She was selected as a foundation bitch for her ability to complement Falkor’s strengths while contributing her own qualities of balance, stability, and breed correctness to future generations.

Pedigree & Background

Evie’s pedigree reflects a thoughtful blend of structure, movement, and stable temperament, emphasizing consistency and long-term soundness over short-term trends.

The lineage behind Evie was selected to preserve correct breed type while supporting a functional, balanced dog capable of producing puppies with both physical soundness and stable dispositions.

As a foundation bitch, Evie represents the cornerstone of the Heartland breeding program, chosen not only for her individual merit but for the reliability and compatibility of the pedigree behind her.

Why This Pairing

The Falkor × Evie pairing was planned with balance, soundness, and long-term breed preservation in mind. Rather than doubling down on extremes, this breeding brings together two dogs whose strengths complement one another both physically and temperamentally.

Falkor contributes strong overall balance, clean structure, and efficient, effortless movement, along with a composed and thoughtful temperament. Evie brings femininity, correct proportions, steadiness, and consistency, serving as a solid foundation for producing puppies that are both structurally sound and mentally stable.

This pairing was selected to preserve correct breed type while prioritizing functional movement, moderation, and reliability. The goal is to produce Manchester Terriers capable of succeeding in the ring while also thriving as companions, performance dogs, or future breeding prospects.

Every breeding decision at Heartland Manchester Terriers is made with the long view in mind—focusing not only on the individual dogs, but on what they can consistently contribute to the breed as a whole.

With this pairing, the goal is to maintain correct structure and sound movement while continuing to strengthen confidence, stability, and overall consistency in future generations.

Breeding Goals & Philosophy

Heartland Manchester Terriers is a small, purpose-driven breeding program focused on preservation, sound structure, stable temperament, and thoughtful placement. Each breeding is planned with intention, emphasizing balance, moderation, and long-term consistency rather than short-term trends.

Dogs are evaluated as complete individuals—considering structure, movement, temperament, and health as interconnected pieces rather than isolated traits. Breeding decisions are made conservatively, with the understanding that preservation requires patience, honesty, and restraint.

The goal at Heartland is not volume or visibility, but quality and responsibility—producing Manchester Terriers capable of thriving as companions, performance partners, and future contributors to the breed.

“Quiet dogs with correct bodies change breeds. Loud dogs with bad movement just win weekends.”
