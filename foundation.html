Foundation Dogs

Our foundation dogs represent the structure, temperament, and health priorities that guide every breeding decision at Heartland Manchester Terriers.

Falkor

GCH Kairos Lucky Dragon at Heartland TKN, BCAT

Health Tested Stud Dog Manchester Terrier

Falkor is a Grand Champion male selected for correct construction, efficient movement, and a steady, sensible temperament. He is a moderate, well-built dog with clean lines and reliable structure that holds up consistently.

Chosen as a foundation dog with long-term preservation in mind, Falkor brings reliability and composure—qualities that support producing correct, stable Manchester Terriers.

Pedigree & Background

Falkor’s pedigree reflects established lines known for sound structure, functional movement, and consistency rather than extremes. His lineage supports thoughtful, sustainable breeding decisions.

Health Testing

Falkor has completed comprehensive health testing appropriate for the Standard Manchester Terrier. Thyroid testing is currently pending and will be updated upon completion.

Standard (Required)

  • von Willebrand’s Disease (vWD): Clear (DNA-based; OFA registered)
  • Autoimmune Thyroiditis: Pending
  • Eye Examination (ACVO): Normal (OFA registered)
  • Cardiac Evaluation (Basic – Cardiologist): Normal
  • Patellar Luxation: Normal (OFA)

Additional Testing

  • Degenerative Myelopathy (DM): Clear (DNA-based)
  • Juvenile Dilated Cardiomyopathy (JDC): Clear (DNA-based)
  • Xanthinuria Type 2A (XUa2): Clear (DNA-based)

View OFA Health Testing

Evie

CH Kairos Magic Mirror at Heartland BCAT

Health Tested Foundation Bitch Manchester Terrier

Evie is a Champion female selected for correct proportions, sound movement, and a calm, confident temperament. She is a balanced bitch who maintains breed type without exaggeration.

Chosen as a foundation bitch, Evie complements Falkor’s strengths while contributing consistency and stability to future generations.

Pedigree & Background

Evie’s pedigree reflects reliable lines known for correct type, soundness, and stable temperaments, supporting long-term breed preservation.

The lineage behind Evie was selected to preserve correct breed type while supporting a functional, balanced dog capable of producing puppies with both physical soundness and stable dispositions.

Health Testing

Evie has completed comprehensive health testing appropriate for the Standard Manchester Terrier. Thyroid testing will be completed after she has finished having puppies.

Standard (Required)

  • von Willebrand’s Disease (vWD): Clear (DNA-based; OFA registered)
  • Autoimmune Thyroiditis: Planned after puppies
  • Eye Examination (ACVO): Normal (OFA registered)
  • Cardiac Evaluation (Basic – Cardiologist): Normal
  • Patellar Luxation: Normal (OFA)

Additional Testing

  • Degenerative Myelopathy (DM): Clear (DNA-based)
  • Juvenile Dilated Cardiomyopathy (JDC): Clear (DNA-based)
  • Xanthinuria Type 2A (XUa2): Clear (DNA-based)

View OFA Health Testing

Why This Pairing

The Falkor × Evie pairing was planned to preserve correct type, sound structure, and stable temperament. Their strengths complement one another, supporting the production of Manchester Terriers suited for the ring, performance, or companion homes.
